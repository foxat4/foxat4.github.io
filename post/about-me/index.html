　　你好！欢迎来玩。我是O。在这里，会不定期更新写作上的小小实验，年底进行年终总结。目前网站还在很初始的阶段，希望之后还会常常摸一摸code进行升级改造。写作语言会以中文为主，但也许什么时候会有英语和正在学习中的日语出没。 　　2021–2025年经常使用微信公众号，大部分内容已经以文字或图像形式搬迁到这里。

　　Hi there! This is O looking to practice and experiment all kinds of writings (one thing for sure is my annual review posted at the end of a year), so consider this blog as a site for exploration. This is just starting, still very much text-based, but hopefully it gets more interactive and fun over time :) my writing will mostly be in Chinese. Perhaps later English and Japanese will come in.
